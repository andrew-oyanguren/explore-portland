Our Picks

Portland Zoo
Approximately 2 miles southwest of downtown Portland. The 64-acre zoo is the oldest zoo west of the Mississippi River.
Multnomah Falls
The beautiful Multnomah Falls is the tallest waterfall in the state of Oregon, and just a 30 minute drive from the city.
Rose Garden
The International Rose Test Garden features more than 10,000 roses. The roses bloom from April through October with the peak coming in June.
Aeriel Tram
One of the only aerial commuter trams in the country, Portland Aerial Tram connects South Waterfront to the main Oregon Health & Science University campus.
